Material Highlights

Key benefits and considerations for each material type

Vinyl Siding

Most popular & budget-friendly

  • Virtually maintenance-free
  • Wide color selection
  • Moisture resistant
  • Can fade in UV

Fiber Cement

Premium durability & performance

  • Fire & pest resistant
  • Withstands harsh weather
  • Authentic wood look
  • Requires repainting

Wood Siding

Natural beauty & timeless appeal

  • Unique natural character
  • Fully customizable
  • Eco-friendly option
  • High maintenance needs

Metal Siding

Modern design & longevity

  • 40-70 year lifespan
  • Fire resistant
  • Contemporary aesthetic
  • Can dent from impact

Composite

Best of all worlds

  • Superior durability
  • Eco-friendly materials
  • Low maintenance
  • Higher upfront cost
